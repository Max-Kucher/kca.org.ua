Accreditation

Kyiv Christian Academy was granted accreditation by one of the major U.S. accrediting agencies, Middle States Association on February 13, 2021. This is a result of the hard work of the KCA staff and parents that comprised the accreditation committees last year. KCA was re-accredited by the Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI). These acknowledgements are for 7 years. We look forward to working with Middle States and to continuing our relationship with ACSI as we strive for excellence.
