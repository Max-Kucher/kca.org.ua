I can't recommend KCA highly enough. This school gives students a real chance to flourish and step into leadership roles in ways that might not be possible at bigger schools. They provided invaluable and real advice which I still hold onto. I have extremely fond memories of the traditions that the school has and I’m so grateful for getting to grow up in an environment like KCA. I wouldn't trade my time there for anything
