Teaching Opportunities

Kyiv Christian Academy, as a K-12 international school, is always looking for dedicated, qualified teachers who love God and wish to serve Him in missions.

Interested applicants are qualified with a Bachelorâ€™s Degree, preferably in the field of Education. State teaching certification or a certificate from ACSI are preferred, but not mandatory.

Teachers raise their own support and are encouraged to come under the auspices of a mission agency. Consider contacting RCE International for more information.

We welcome the participation of retired teachers, those who have recently graduated, and those with experience in public and private schools. Come to Kyiv for a life-changing experience in serving the Lord!

We are in need of most positions with staff arriving on campus by mid-August. All who are interested are welcome to apply.

  • Lead Teacher
  • Elementary teachers
  • 6th grade teachers (English, math, science, history, Bible)
  • Enrichments teachers (art, music, PE, computer)
  • ESL Teachers
